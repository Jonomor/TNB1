The current market dislocation, evidenced by XRP's 14.14% weekly depreciation to $1.6500, presents a critical divergence between speculative price action and underlying network utility. The current market dynamics, while challenging for short-term holders, are structurally beneficial for long-term utility adoption, preparing the asset for its intended function as a bridge currency in a de-risked financial ecosystem.
